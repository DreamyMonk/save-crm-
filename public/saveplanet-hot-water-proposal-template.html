WHO WE ARE
About SavePlanet

SavePlanet is a leading provider of energy-efficient solutions specialising in heat pump hot water systems, solar hot water, rooftop solar PV, lithium home batteries, and comprehensive energy-saving technologies across Victoria and New South Wales.

Operating as an authorised representative under the Victorian Energy Upgrades program, we ensure all hot water installations meet stringent VEU compliance for both Activity 35 (Solar Hot Water) and Activity 36 (Heat Pump Hot Water). With specialised expertise in modern hot water technology — CO₂ refrigerant heat pumps, evacuated tube solar collectors, and smart thermostatic systems — we deliver cutting-edge, cost-effective upgrades that maximise efficiency and reduce running costs by up to 75% compared to old electric or gas storage tanks.

Scope of Work

Pre-Installation Assessment
On-site survey: existing tank inspection, electrical/plumbing supply check, household demand sizing, location assessment for the new unit, and confirmation of VEU activity eligibility (35 or 36) before commencement.

THE TECHNOLOGY
How Heat Pump Hot Water Works
VEU ACTIVITY 36 · APPROVED TECHNOLOGY
Heat Pump Hot Water
A heat pump is essentially a reverse air-conditioner for your hot water — it captures free heat from the surrounding air and uses it to warm the water in your tank. Because it moves heat instead of generating it, a heat pump uses up to 3× less electricity than a traditional electric storage tank, drastically cutting your bill while delivering reliable hot water 24/7.
