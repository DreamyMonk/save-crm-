WHO WE ARE
About SavePlanet

SavePlanet is a leading provider of energy-efficient solutions specialising in rooftop solar PV systems, lithium home battery storage, hybrid inverters, smart energy management, and complete grid-export solutions across Victoria and New South Wales.

Operating as an authorised representative under the Victorian Energy Upgrades program and as a Clean Energy Council (CEC) accredited installer, we ensure all activities meet stringent VEU, STC, and CEC compliance requirements. We manage VEEC certificate creation, STC submission, DNSP grid-connection approval, and regulatory oversight end-to-end.

With specialised expertise in solar generation and battery storage technologies, we deliver cutting-edge, cost-effective renewable systems that maximise self-consumption, reduce grid dependency, and accelerate ROI. Our team designs every system around your roof, shading, load profile, and future EV-charging needs.

Serving over 800 happy households across Victoria, we take pride in delivering tangible bill savings directly to our customers while making measurable contributions to carbon reduction.
