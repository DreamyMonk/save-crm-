What We Deliver

Scope of Work

The proposed space heating and cooling upgrade includes the following four-stage delivery, executed by accredited installers under VEU compliance protocols.

01

Pre-Installation Assessment

Our qualified team conducts a thorough site assessment to calculate load, verify suitability, suggest the appropriate heating/cooling system, assess electrical supply adequacy, and confirm compliance with all applicable standards and building regulations before commencement.

02

Professional Installation

Our expert technicians install the new space heating and cooling system at your premises, ensuring it meets all safety and efficiency standards. All workmanship is carried out by licensed, accredited installers under AS/NZS 3000.

03

Decommissioning & Removal

We safely decommission and remove the old heating/cooling unit. Where the old product is not safe or practical to remove, the heating element/burner/electrical component is rendered inoperable per VEU regulations before the new install proceeds.

04

System Commissioning

Following installation, we fully commission the heating-cooling system — including performance testing, temperature verification, and operational checks — to ensure optimal efficiency and functionality before handover.
